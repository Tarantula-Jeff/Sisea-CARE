Donation of Exam Materials

Project Overview

Sis3aCARE successfully donated examination materials to Lolobi Ashiambi LA JHS, Akpafu Todzi JHS, Santrokofi Gbodome and Likpe koforidua JHS to support students in preparing for their assessments. The initiative provided mathematical sets, pens, and rulers, helping to create a more conducive environment for learning and examinations. This project aims to boost student performance and confidence by ensuring they have the essential tools needed for success. The effort was made possible through the generosity of Mr. Francis Numor and the collaboration of the school administration, whose commitment to education made this achievement possible. Through this initiative, Sis3aCARE continues to empower students and foster equal opportunities for academic growth and a brighter future.

  • Start Date:30th May 2025
  • Target Completion: 30th May 2025
  • Communities Covered: 4
  • Estimated Reach: 100+ students
