School Sanitation Support–WasteBin Distribution

Project Overview

As part of our commitment to creating safe and healthy learning environments, Sis3aCARE implemented the School Sanitation Support Project by distributing dustbins to Lolobi Ashiambi RC Primary School. The objective of this initiative was to promote environmental cleanliness and proper waste management among young learners, fostering a culture of responsibility and sustainability within the school. By improving the school’s sanitation facilities, we helped create a cleaner, safer learning space for both students and teachers.

  • Start Date:January 2025
  • Target Completion:January 2025
  • Communities Covered: 1
